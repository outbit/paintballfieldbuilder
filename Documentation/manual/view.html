Paintball Field Builder Help

View Options and Settings



-Changing Views-

To change a view click Top, First Person, or Perspective under the View Options.




The Top view allows you to see the entire field from a strait down view.

The First Person view allows you to see the entire field as if you were actually walking it.

The Perspective view allows you to orbit around the field and get a good angled view of it.



-Changing View Options-

The view options allow you to customize how the scene is displayed. Many of the settings may improve or decrease performance.



Terrain toggles if the terrain is visible or not. You may not place objects when the terrain is not visible.

Yard Lines toggles if the yardline grid is visible.

SkyBox toggles if the background is sky or just white.

50 Yard Line toggles if the 50 yard line is visible.

Center Yard Line toggles if the center yard line is visible.

Shadows toggles if the bunkers cast shadows or not.

Gouraud Shading toggles if smooth shading is on.

Texture Mapping toggles if texture mapping is enabled.

Solid View toggles if the scene is rendered solid or wireframe.
